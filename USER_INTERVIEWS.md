# User Interview Summary

This document summarizes discussions and observations related to AI subscription spending and cost optimization challenges faced by users.

## Target Users

 Students using AI tools
 Freelancers
 Developers
 Small teams
 Startup founders
 Content creators


## Common Problems

 Users subscribe to expensive plans unnecessarily
 Difficulty comparing plans across providers
 Lack of visibility into monthly AI expenses
 Confusion between similar subscription tiers
 Teams paying for features they do not use
