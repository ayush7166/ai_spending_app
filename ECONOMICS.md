# Economic Analysis

This document explains the financial and operational benefits of the AI Spend Audit Engine.


## Problem

Organizations and individuals increasingly subscribe to multiple AI tools without analyzing whether those subscriptions are cost-efficient.

Many users overpay for features they do not fully utilize.


## Economic Impact

The application helps users:

 Reduce unnecessary subscription spending
 Identify lower-cost alternatives
 Improve software budget allocation
 Optimize per-user AI expenses
